Frequently Asked Questions

Often, students will have some questions pop up. Maybe a few of these will help you finding your way at HZ.

  • How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    • Use https://print.hz.nl, log in and upload the file(s) that you want to print. Now find a printer and use your student card to log in and select print.

  • How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

    • At an printer of HZ use your student card and select scan, now your files will be scanned.

  • How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

  • Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

    • Click here to view the latest updates about COVID-19 regulations in The Netherlands.

  • How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

    • On the home page on the Self Service Portal you should choose Reservations & visitors. From there you are able to book a room.

  • What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    • You can park your car for free (make sure to have your Student Card with you) near the APV Campus, Middelburg, at Poelendaesingel 58.

  • How can I get a Facility Card?

    • You should contact the Study Coach of our section, Brigitte Pommee.

  • How can I play ''tafelvoetbal'' (=dutch)tablesoccer at HZ?

    • You have to go to ground floor (level 0 is what we call it in the Netherlands) and ask for the tablesoccer ball at the reception. Make sure to bring it back!.

  • How can I make sure my Github is up to date?

    • Do not forget to commit your files to your repository, by using the command git commit -m "status update message in here" and pushing it to the net by using -git push.
